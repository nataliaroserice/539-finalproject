About This Project

This website is inspired by a poster I designed for a graphic design course. I developed an A-Z list of 26 low(ish) maintenance plants, illustrated each by hand, researched their care needs, and placed them all into a modular grid. Since then, I've considered how I could continue building on it: Stickers? Prints?


Then, when deciding on a final project for my Web Design course, I realized the plant poster could make for a joyful and potentially useful online resource. So, I decided to turn it into an interactive guide for people who love the idea of plants but need forgiving options.


Whether you're figuring out what kind of light your apartment gets or just want something that won't react dramatically in the face of infrequent watering, I hope this helps you find a plant that aligns with your lifestyle and commitment level.
